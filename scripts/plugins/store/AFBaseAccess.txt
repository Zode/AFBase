#AFBase access file, everyone has flag Z by default.
#Flags
#A - Immunity
#B - Set access, stop/start expansion
#C - Rcon
#D - Ban/unban
#E - Kick, changelevel, "highrisk"
#F - Ent_ commands
#G - Player_ commands, slap, slay, trackdecals
#H - Fun_commands, say, vote
#IJKLMNOPQRSTUVWXY - Custom access flags 1 to 17

#Format
#STEAMID ACCESSFLAGS

#Examples
#STEAM_0:1:129159025 ABCDEFGHIJKLMNOPQRSTUVWYX
#STEAM_0:1:129159025 CDEFGHI
#STEAM_0:1:129159025 Y
